IAM is responsible for managing:  

* **Users** and/or **user groups**  
* **Roles**  
* **Policies**  

---

### Users  

* Definitions and permissions for an **individual user**.  

### Groups  

* Definitions and permissions for a **group of users**.  

### Roles  

* Definitions and permissions for **AWS services**.
